Welcome

SCP: Anomalies is a mod that adds most of the various SCPs that are usually rarely known by many people. This mod is also inspired by the SCP wiki, SCP Unity and various Mods from the SCP Containment Breach game such as Terror Hunt Mod, Ultimate Edition and many more.
